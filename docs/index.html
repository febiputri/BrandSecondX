The second brand is a marketplace or the newest place to buy and sell that provides various kinds of used goods that are still fit for use. used goods will usually be thrown away, gifted or just stored because they are not used. However, some people may use it as a source to make money. More than 75 percent of people say that getting extra money is the main reason Indonesians sell preloved goods. This is an inspiration for sellers to sell their goods, so that used goods that are no longer used but still fit for use can make money, as well as buyers, can fulfill customer desires.
